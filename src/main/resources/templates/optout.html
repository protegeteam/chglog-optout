WebProtege De-identified Obfuscated Change Log Publication Opt-Out

This page allows you to opt out of the publication of de-identified and obfuscated change logs for some or all of your WebProtégé projects.

Please check that your user name and your email address are correct. Select the projects for which you do not want a de-identified and obfuscated version of the project change log to appear in the published dataset.

Use the check boxes to select some or all of your projects. Selected projects will be excluded from the dataset that we will publish.
